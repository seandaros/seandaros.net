A Digital Archive for SEAN MICHAEL DA ROS
I don't like the term photographer because when I hear it I think of a paparazzo. I prefer the term photojournalist; I like to document both my life and my family's. I understand the importance of photo sequencing, but I prefer to keep the original order of photos. Every photo is organized into a gallery. If I don't have enough photos for a gallery, the photos aren't published. I don't believe in individual photos, I believe in galleries of several photos that tell a story of what you were witnessing. That's my reason for taking pictures; to tell a story.
